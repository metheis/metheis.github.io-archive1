Foundation

We were founded on the principle that every human being has the fundimental right to learn to read. It does not matter his speed, race, language, culutre, or ethnicity, everyone should have the opportunity.

Mark Theis - Founder

Diagnosed with Dyslexia at a young age, Mark has overcome obsticals time and again. After acconplishing high ambitions throughout his educational carrier, he started Reading Right with the goal to help others with learning differences to achieve their goals.
