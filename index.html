The project

Music is essential to just about everyone's life. It defines cultural trends and social issues, but also unspoken moods and complications. Being able to create music is freedom to express oneself. And what is better than creating the instrument that creates music?

I will build a mountain dulcimer. A string instrument originating from the Appalachian Mountains, dulcimers were first commonly built in the 1700s. A beautiful instrument to both admire and listen, I have chosen this as a culmination of my interests in woodworking and music.

Learn everything you can, anytime you can, from anyone you can, there will always come a time when you will be grateful you did.
