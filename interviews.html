Father Martin

February XX, 2018

Father Martin is the head monk at Priory School. He has been a part of the community since 1973, and he has built with wood since he lived on the East Coast. He has built over 21 dulcimers, and he is my mentor in the building process.

Scott Walker

February XX, 2018

Scott began tinkering with electronics at an early age. In his early teens he began playing guitar and experimenting with pickups. Fifteen years later he found himself working alongside luthier Richard Hoover, of Santa Cruz Guitar Company, studying acoustic guitar construction. At Santa Cruz Guitar Company he hand carved over 1000 necks. Today he owns his own shop and builds electric guitars.
