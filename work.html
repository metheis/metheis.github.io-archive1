Our Work

Our organizaiton educates young minds that work differently.

Teaching Differently

Every person learns differently, some more than others. There is a method of teaching that works effectivly for every person. The key is to find what resonates, and foster that pathway

Slingerland®

The Slingerland® approach has been shown to foster and assit a strong foundation for literacy. It entails oral and written language skills in the classroom for all types of learners, especially those with dyslexia. Our teachers have been trained to use the Slingerland® structure, stratagies, and tools for effective instruction.

Digital Style

English contains 26 characters that tend to blend, jump, and move around on the page for dyslexics. Research has proven changing fonts, character size, and spacing enables for improved reading accuracy and speed. ReadignRight.org utilizes a 100% digital envirenment for students in order to customize page text, colors, and layouts.
